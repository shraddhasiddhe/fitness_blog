Healthy Recipe of the Week

This week's healthy recipe is a delicious quinoa salad with mixed vegetables. It's packed with flavor and nutrients!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse quinoa under cold water.
  2. In a pot, combine quinoa and water. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce to a simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Fluff quinoa with a fork and let it cool.
  4. In a large bowl, combine cooled quinoa, bell pepper, cucumber, and tomatoes.
  5. Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour over salad and toss to combine.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
